About PPA

Party Popularity Analyzer is a project launched by Mexican students @ Tec de Monterrey. Our goal is to give a glance to the population of Mexico and the world of how each candidate and party reflects their popularity based on their tweets.
We are using the Twitter API and some technologies like Flask, Mongo and Python.
